Hi, I'm Molly Jean.

I’m a JavaScript developer based in Portland, Oregon. I believe that building things is all about getting up & falling & getting up again & falling better & iterating & improving & getting it right.

My approach to problem solving is informed by my background in writing and non-profit admin. Whether I’m trying to get the right quote for a story or refactor a React component, I’ve found that resilience, attention to detail, and ferocious curiosity are my greatest strengths.

A failed approach is not a defeat, but an opportunity to research, learn more, and try again. In both writing and web development, asking good questions is essential—not just the “how” but also the “why.”
